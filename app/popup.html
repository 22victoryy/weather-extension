Who am I?

Hello, welcome to my personal website!  I am Victor Cho, a second year student studying at the University of Toronto, working towards an Honours of Bachelor of Science in Computational Cognition and Statistics. I am currently enrolled in a Cognitive Science major and a Statistics major. I enjoy observing data and programmig, especially in projects where I am given the opportunity of listening to other people's ideas while expressing my own to create a product with integrated ideas. As a person of a third cultural background, whether it would be from simply working on my own or with other people, I value efficiency, motivation, and communication.  ​ My future goal for my career is to integrate my statistical, computational cognitive science background in such fields such as quantitative research or data science.
